About Us!

Here at Chicken Lovers, we're dedicated to bringing you tasty, quality meals at an affordable price point. We char-grill our chicken for every order. You won't find any frozen or microwaved ingredients here. Not in the mood for chicken? Check out our extensive menu of ribs, burgers, and other options and we guarantee that you'll find something that calls your tastebuds into action.
