Patricia was born at the now defunct St. Vincent's Hospital in Manhattan during the Blizzard of '88. Her father broke several laws driving her mother from Queens to Manhattan. Her life has taken many paths and detour since that fateful drive.

She attended Stony Brook University intending to major in biochemistry but ended up dropping the her second semester because she couldn't deal with calculus. She decided to channel her passion for the written word into a totally real life application and switched her major to English. She graduated cum laude in 2012.
